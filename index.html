The more accessible way to ask for a phone number

Over the years phones have become a more versatile mode of communication then they used to be. We use them both for oral and text based communication (and have been doing so for a while now).

And although there are a lot of people who can (or prefer to) use only one of both forms, when we ask for phone numbers in analog or digital forms, we often don't give the formfiller the chance to tell us for which forms(oral or written) we can use their phone number.
